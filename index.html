Our Story

Homestyle Cooking Crafted for Sharing

Jeana's Kitchen is a family-run kitchen based in Malabon City, known for generous party trays, delightful cakes, and fully-customized catering spreads. From everyday gatherings to milestone events, we prepare comfort dishes and desserts that travel beautifully and taste like they were made at home.
